Wavey Baby Blanket

Need to finish by November 1, 2023

A present for my cousin Kara's second kid. It is another girl.
This blanket is made with Brava Bulky Yarn, a 100% acrylic yarn. To account for any allergies, all my knitted baby items are made with acrylic yarn.

Waves and Ripples Blanket Pattern
The blanket is made with 3 skeins of Seraphim and 2 skeins of Dove and size US 10/6.00mm needles.

Pattern

CO 130 sts
Work three rows garter stitch. Then switch to pattern below in alternating color stripes:

  1. K1, K1fb, K3, SSK, K2tog, K3, K1fb, Rep from to last st, K1
  2. Purl

When reach desired length work three rows of garter stitch and BO.
